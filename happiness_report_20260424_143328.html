📝 Methodology Notes

😊 Happiness Index: % of posts containing at least one smile emoji (😊 😀 😁 😄 😃 🙂 ☺️ 😸 😺 🥰 😍 😌 🤗 😏) — direct positive sentiment signal
🎉 Vitality Index: % of posts containing party/gathering keywords (party, celebration, bbq, potluck, gathering, festival, etc.) — reflects social activeness, not directly happiness
These are two independent indexes — they are intentionally not combined into a single score
Data source: Reddit public JSON API, 500 most recent posts per city subreddit
Limitation: Reddit skews younger and more vocal — treat as one indicator among many
