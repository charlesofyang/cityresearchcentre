📝 Methodology & Definitions

😊 Happiness Index: A simplified proxy for positive sentiment based on observable indicators within online content.
🎉 Vitality Index: A proxy for social activity based on signals related to gatherings, events, and community interaction.
These are two independent indexes — intentionally never combined into a single score.
Data source: Publicly available online community posts via public API. 500 most recent posts per city.
Limitation: Online data is self-selected and may not represent the demographics or views of the total population.
