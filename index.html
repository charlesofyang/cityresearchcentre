About

What we do

We collect and analyse publicly available social media data to produce two independent indexes tracking how communities express themselves online over time.

📡

Data Collection

We gather publicly available posts from online community forums on a quarterly basis, analysing digital sentiment signals across city communities.

📊

Independent Indexes

Two separate indexes — Happiness and Vitality — are produced independently and never combined into a single score.

🗓️

Quarterly Reports

Our Indexes

Two independent measures

We deliberately keep our two indexes separate, as they measure fundamentally different aspects of community life.

😊
Happiness Index
A simplified proxy for positive sentiment based on observable indicators within online content. Measures how frequently city communities express positive emotion in their digital communications.
🎉
Vitality Index
A proxy for social activity based on signals related to gatherings, events, and community interaction. Reflects a community's social activeness — intentionally kept separate from the Happiness Index.
⚠️ Interpretation Note: These indexes are simplified indicators derived from limited online activity and should not be interpreted as direct measurements of real-world happiness, quality of life, or overall community conditions.
