How to read these reports

📈

Trends matter more than absolutes

Changes between quarterly reports are more meaningful than any single score. Look for consistent movement over time.

⚖️

Two indexes are independent

The Happiness and Vitality indexes are never combined. A city can score high on one and low on the other.

🔍

One signal among many

Use these indexes as one informal reference point, not a definitive measure of any city's quality of life.

👥

Online ≠ everyone

Data reflects online community activity only. Residents who don't participate online are not represented.
